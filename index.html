About the Convening

The ICDDS 2026 brings together leading experts, researchers, and policymakers from across the globe to address the critical challenges of diarrheal disease surveillance.

  • Scalable diagnostic solutions for enteric pathogens
  • Optimal control strategies and vaccine implementation
  • Sustainability of global surveillance networks
